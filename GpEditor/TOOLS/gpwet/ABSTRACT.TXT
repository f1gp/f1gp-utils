Title   : Grand Prix/World Circuit Wet Race Patch (IBM)
Keywords: GRAND PRIX WORLD CIRCUIT F1SA F1GP GP WC WET RACE

GpWet (V1.1) patches GP.EXE (USA/English/French/German/Italian V1.05) and
modifies the probability of a wet race. Normally there is a 6.25% chance
of a wet race. Using GpWet you can set the probability anywhere from
always wet (100%) to always dry (0%).

Freeware contributed by the author Trevor Kellaway (CIS:100331,2330). This
is *not* an MPS product. Requires PKUNZIP v2.04g to decompress.
