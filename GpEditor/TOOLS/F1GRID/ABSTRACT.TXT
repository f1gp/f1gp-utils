F1Grid V1.0 allows you to setup the entire grid for a race in the Microprose
F1 Grand Prix (World Circuit) simulator (US & European V1.05). F1Grid allows
you to edit the car positions for a saved qualifying session.

Freeware contributed by the author Trevor Kellaway (CIS:100331,2330). This
is not an MPS product. Requires PKUNZIP 2.04g to decompress.
