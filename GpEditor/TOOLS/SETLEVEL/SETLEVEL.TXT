SetLevel V1.0 14/Jun/95 - Set Grand Prix level for a saved game.
Copyright (c) T.Kellaway (CIS:100331,2330) 1994 - All Rights Reserved.

SetLevel allows you to modify the game level for a saved game in the
MPS Grand Prix/World Circuit simulation (V1.05 US & European).

SetLevel is mainly intended for modifying the game level for a championship
season whilst part of the way through a season.
 
Usage: SetLevel (saved game) (level)
          1 - Rookie
          2 - Amateur
          3 - Semi-Pro
          4 - Pro
          5 - Ace
